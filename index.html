01 — Concept

Tea as a moment, not a habit

ATEATUDE reframes tea drinking as a ritual. Instead of speed and distraction, the app supports presence: what you taste, how you feel, and what you want to remember.

Ritual-first Taste notes Mood + reflection Minimal UI

03 — Design choices

Calm typography, high clarity

Playfair Display for expressive headings, Poppins for clean readability. Spacing stays generous to keep cognitive load low.

Hierarchy

Clear H1/H2 rhythm + short paragraphs.

Color

Primary base + green accents, no harsh contrast.

Motion

Soft, slow micro-interactions. Nothing flashy.
