02 — Problem & context

Tea is often consumed without attention.

With so many different teas available, people lose track of what they like, what they don’t, and what they want to try next.

Solution: ATEATUDE brings the focus back to tea.

A calm, distraction-free app that supports mindful tea moments, not social performance.

Who

Tea enjoyers of any age. People who see tea as a calm, intentional moment and want to keep track of what they enjoy, without social pressure.

Why now

We live in a fast, always-on world where even calm moments are distracted. At the same time, interest in tea and slow rituals is growing, but there is no digital space that supports this mindset without social noise.

03 — Concept

ATEATUDE is a calm tea journal.

The app turns drinking tea into an intentional ritual instead of a background habit. Users can log the tea they drink, discover new teas, and save them for later — all in a quiet, distraction-free environment.

Target audience

Tea enjoyers of any age. People who see tea as a calm, intentional moment and want to keep track of what they enjoy, without social pressure.

Value proposition

ATEATUDE offers a calm, distraction-free way to enjoy and remember tea.
